Homecoming: A Film by Beyoncé

Rating: ★★★★★


Director:

Beyoncé, Ed Burke


Description:

This intimate, in-depth look at Beyoncé's celebrated 2018 Coachella performance reveals the emotional road from creative concept to cultural movement.


My reason for recommending:

As a Beyoncé's fan, this documentary shows us more of her off-stage preparation, emotions, and struggles. I'm able to know a more in-depth and intimate story of her as a person. Her performance is HISTORIC!
