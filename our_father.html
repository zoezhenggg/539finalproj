Our Father

Rating: ★★★☆☆


Director:

Lucie Jourdan


Description:

After a woman's at-home DNA test reveals multiple half-siblings, she discovers a shocking scheme involving donor sperm and a popular fertility doctor.


My reason for recommending:

This is a very interesting documentary. It let me know a dark side of humanity. It's just unbelievable that a medical doctor could conduct this kind of crime. At the end of the documentary, it questions the integrity of our law, since there's no law that targets on this kind of crime. Overall, I enjoyed watching this documentary.
