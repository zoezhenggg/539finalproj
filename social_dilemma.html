The Social Dilemma

Rating: ★★★★☆


Director:

Jeff Orlowski-Yang


Description:

Explores the dangerous human impact of social networking; tech experts sound the alarm on their own creations.


My reason for recommending:

I love this documentary. It really provides me information about the Internet, algorithms, and perspectives from tech people. It really is a great documentary that leads us to think about humanity, the development of technology, and ethics around it. I really recommend this documentary.
